Workflow

How CoreGuard works from start to finish.

The process is intentionally simple: assign a plan, complete the session, review the summary, and return clear feedback.

1

Create or receive a plan

The physio assigns a rehab plan with selected exercises and optional analysis requirements.

2

Track rehab progress

The client logs pain level and notes while following the assigned plan.

3

Run live squat analysis

Camera-based tracking checks simple rules for knee position, depth, and forward lean.

4

Review and share feedback

The physio reviews the summary, finalises comments, and sends them back to the client.
