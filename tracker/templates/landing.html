Movement Analysis

Designed to make movement review easy to understand at a glance.

CoreGuard uses a clear movement-tracking view to support squat analysis during live sessions. This helps the platform present posture and joint alignment in a way that feels intuitive for both physiotherapists and clients.

Instant camera connection Starts from the user’s device camera so a live session can begin quickly with very little setup.
Live movement tracking Follows the squat position in real time to support rule checks around depth, posture, and knee alignment.
Simple client experience Keeps the process easy to use so clients can complete a session and return later for physiotherapist feedback.
