FAQ & Support

Helpful answers for the CoreGuard workflow

This page explains the most important parts of the system clearly, including where feedback appears, how live analysis works, and what to expect when a plan requires video analysis.

Where do I see physiotherapist feedback?

Feedback appears in the analysis session summary. Clients can open a completed session from their plan page and read the physiotherapist comments once they have been shared.

How does live analysis work?

The client starts a live squat analysis session through a plan that requires it. CoreGuard processes the camera feed, creates summary metrics, and stores the session so it can be reviewed later by the physiotherapist.

What if my plan needs video analysis?

Plans that require live analysis show a dedicated action to start the session. Once the session is completed, it becomes available in the plan area and can later include physiotherapist feedback.

What does the physiotherapist review?

The physiotherapist can review frame counts, flagged movement rules, average angle metrics, and any AI-assisted feedback draft before sending final comments to the client.

What is stored in a rehabilitation plan?

Each plan stores its title, duration in weeks, notes, whether live analysis is required, and the exercise prescription including sets and reps for each exercise.

Need Direction?

Quick guidance by role

For physiotherapists

Create a plan, review submitted sessions, and share final feedback from the analysis summary page.

For clients

Open your plan, complete any required analysis session, then return later to the summary page to view shared feedback.
